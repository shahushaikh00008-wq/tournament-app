TOURNAMENT RULES

Please Read All Rules Carefully Before Registering

General Rules

  • Every player must have a valid Free Fire account.
  • Only registered players are allowed to participate.
  • Players must join the custom room at least 15 minutes before the match starts.
  • Room ID and Password will be shared before the match.
  • Respect admins, referees, and other players.
  • Use your original in-game name during the tournament.

Gameplay Rules

  • Battle Royale - Squad Mode.
  • Maximum 4 players per team.
  • No substitute player without admin approval.
  • No teaming with another squad.
  • Intentional suicide is not allowed.
  • Disconnects are considered part of the game.

Fair Play Rules

  • Hack, Cheat, Script, Injector or Third-Party Apps are strictly prohibited.
  • No Emulator Players (Mobile Only).
  • VPN usage is not allowed.
  • Exploiting game bugs will result in disqualification.
  • Unsportsmanlike behavior may lead to a permanent ban.

Esports Rules

  • All players must follow official esports fair play standards.
  • Any form of match-fixing is strictly prohibited.
  • Stream sniping is not allowed.
  • Voice communication must remain respectful.
  • Abusive language, racism, or harassment will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Admin decisions are final and cannot be challenged during the tournament.
  • Prize money will be awarded only after verification.
  • Players must provide proof of identity if requested.
  • Every player must use their own account.
  • Sharing accounts is prohibited.

Streaming Rules

  • Streaming is allowed with a minimum 3-minute delay.
  • Do not reveal Room ID or Password during live streaming.
  • Recording matches is recommended.
  • Tournament organizers may stream your gameplay.

Disqualification Reasons

  • Using Hacks or Cheats.
  • Using Emulator.
  • Fake Registration Information.
  • Not Joining on Time.
  • Abusing Admins or Players.
  • Sharing Room Password.
  • Intentional Teaming.
  • Ignoring Tournament Rules.

Prize Distribution

  • 1st Place - ₹5,000 + Trophy
  • 2nd Place - ₹3,000
  • 3rd Place - ₹2,000
  • Prize money will be transferred within 7 working days.
Breaking Any Rule Can Result In Immediate Disqualification Without Refund.
